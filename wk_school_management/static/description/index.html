Odoo Education Management

A comprehensive yet simplified education management software built for all educational institutes.
Odoo All-in-One Education Management System is a complete, well-designed software to help run an educational organization(school, institute, college) easier. From student portals, enrollments, and class schedules to teacher management and reporting, this software integrates every educational administrative function smoothly.

All-in-One School Dashboard

Gain full control with a unified dashboard that centralizes school activities, offering a quick overview of student attendance, faculty availability, and assignments. Real-time insights enable administrators to make informed decisions efficiently.

Smart Enrollment and Applications Management

Simplify admissions with our smart enrollment system. It streamlines applications, tracks enrollment status, and organizes records, letting schools focus on education instead of paperwork.

Efficient Class and Student Management

Streamline academic operations with class and student management. This module organizes schedules, tracks performance, and manages grades and attendance for an efficient learning environment.

Attendance Tracking System

Efficiently monitor attendance with our module that offers detailed reports. It tracks two types: daily school attendance for entry and class attendance for individual classes, ensuring accurate student engagement.

Enrollments

Classes

Timetable

Students

Teachers

Organized management of teacher profiles, including subject assignments, schedules, and performance tracking.

Assignments

This shows all students with their assigned tasks, allowing the admin to submit assignments directly. It includes details like marks, term, exam type, subject, percentage, and assignment status (new, submitted, evaluated).

Dashboard

The student dashboard provides all essential information, including personal details, overall attendance, fee summary, upcoming classes, notifications, grade summary, total service hours, and the latest blogs.

Timetable

Students can view their timetable in a calendar format or a detailed format , allowing them to see scheduled classes for the day, week, or month based on their preference.
Previous Next

Assignments

Students can view all assigned tasks, search by name or status (to-do, submitted, or evaluated), access assignment details with attachments, and submit their assignments directly from the platform.
Previous Next

Attendance

Students can track their daily attendance in calendar and detailed views, displaying presence and absences. They can also see upcoming public holidays, with color codes: green for present, red for absent, and blue for holidays.

Grades

Students can view a comprehensive grade summary for all subjects in one place, including information on both completed and incomplete assignments.

Transport

Students can view their assigned transport routes, including pickup and drop-off points, vehicle details, and driver information, directly from the platform.
They can also track schedule changes, view route timings, and stay informed about transport availability or updates.

Transcripts

Service Hours

  • Centralizes student profiles, including personal details and academic records.

Centralized Dashboard Overview

  • Provides a comprehensive view of school operations.
  • Offers real-time updates on critical data like attendance, assignments and schedules.
  • Enables quick insights for informed decision-making.
  • Features easy navigation and actionable shortcuts for efficiency.

Class Schedule Management

  • Facilitates the creation of custom timetables.
  • Enables easy allocation of teachers and rooms.
  • Ensures well-organized class schedules.

Comprehensive Attendance Tracking

  • Monitors attendance through daily school entry and individual class tracking.
  • Allows detailed attendance reports and custom status tags.
  • Supports administrators and teachers in maintaining precise records.
  • Enhances student engagement monitoring.

Teacher Management System

  • Centralizes management of teacher information.
  • Tracks subject assignments, schedules, and performance records.
  • Simplifies the process for teachers to request and record time off.

Adaptable Grade Management System

  • Customizes grading systems to fit school policies.
  • Adjusts assignment weights and sets unique grading scales.
  • Supports administrators and teachers in maintaining precise records.
  • Tracks student progress efficiently.

Dynamic Assignment Management

  • Provides a centralized view of all assigned tasks.
  • Allows for easy access to assignment details and status.
  • Enables students to submit assignments directly.
  • Assists teachers in efficiently tracking and managing assignments.

School Announcements Board

  • Allows administrators and teachers to post announcements and updates easily.
  • Enables targeted communication to specific groups (students, parents, teachers).
  • It keeps the school community informed and engaged.
  • A central hub for all school communications.

Service Hours Tracking

  • Allows students to record and monitor their community service hours.

  • Generates detailed academic transcripts for students.

  • Facilitates easy access to academic history for future opportunities.
